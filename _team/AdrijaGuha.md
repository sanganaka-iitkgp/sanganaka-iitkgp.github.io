I am currently pursuing my MS (by Research) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, where I am associated with the  CNeRG Lab under the guidance of Prof. Pawan Goyal. I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
My current research focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Sanskrit Computational Linguistics, Knowledge Graphs, and Large Language Models (LLMs). I am particularly interested in developing knowledge-grounded AI systems for low-resource languages, with ongoing work in Sanskrit computational linguistics and domain-specific question answering for Ayurveda.
Research Interests: Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), Knowledge Graphs, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Sanskrit Computational Linguistics, Low-Resource Language Processing, Information Extraction, Explainable AI, AI for Healthcare.
